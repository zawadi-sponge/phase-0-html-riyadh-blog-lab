History

Riyadh (pronounced "the Gardens) is the capital and largest city of Saudi Arabia. It is also the capital of Riyadh Province, and belongs to the historical regions of Najad and Al-Yamama. It is situated in the center of the Arabia Peninsula on a large plateau, and is home to 5,254,560 people,and the urban center of a region with a population of close to 7 million people

The city is divided into 15 municipal districts, managed by Riyadh Municipality headed by the mayor of Riyadh,and the Riyadh Development Authority, chaired by thr governor of Riyadh Province,Khalid bin Bandar Al Saud.The current mayor of Riyadh is Abdul Rahman Al Mogbel,appointed in 2012.Riyadh has the largest all female university in the world,the princess Nora bint Abdulrahman.[2] It has been designated as a Beta World City
